üí° Understanding Screen Reader Output

  • Screen readers announce element type (heading, link, button, etc.)
  • They specify the heading level (H1, H2, etc.) for proper document structure
  • Interactive elements are identified as "clickable"
  • Form fields announce their type (text field, checkbox, etc.) and whether they're required
  • Images announce their alt text - this is why descriptive alt text is crucial!
  • Lists announce the number of items they contain

üìö Understanding Contrast Requirements

  • WCAG AA (Standard): Minimum legal requirement for most websites
  • WCAG AAA (Enhanced): Higher standard for maximum accessibility
  • Normal Text: Text smaller than 18pt (or 14pt bold)
  • Large Text: Text 18pt and larger (or 14pt+ bold)
  • Why it matters: Users with low vision, color blindness, or viewing in bright sunlight need sufficient contrast to read content
